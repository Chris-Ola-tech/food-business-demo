    FAQ

    Frequently Asked Questions

    Have questions? We've got answers. Find everything you need to know about our service.

    How do I place an order?

    To place an order, simply add the dishes you want to your cart and click the "Checkout via WhatsApp" button. Your order details including items, quantities, and total will automatically appear in a WhatsApp message. Provide your delivery location and let us know if you'll pay via bank transfer or cash on delivery. We'll confirm your order and prepare it for delivery.

    Do you accept cash on delivery?

    Yes, we accept both cash on delivery and bank transfer. You can choose your preferred payment method when placing your order through WhatsApp. For cash on delivery, simply have the exact amount ready when our delivery partner arrives. Bank transfer details will be provided during order confirmation.

    What are your delivery hours?

    We deliver daily from 10:00 AM to 10:00 PM. Orders placed after 9:30 PM will be processed the next day. Delivery times typically range from 30-60 minutes depending on your location and order volume. We'll provide an estimated delivery time when you place your order.

    What areas do you deliver to?

    We currently deliver to all major areas within Ibadan and surrounding neighborhoods. When you place your order via WhatsApp, simply share your delivery address and we'll confirm if we service your area. We're constantly expanding our delivery zones to serve more customers.

    Is there a minimum order amount?

    Yes, we have a minimum order amount of ₦2,500 to ensure efficient delivery service. This helps us maintain quality standards and fair pricing for all customers. You can mix and match any items from our menu to meet the minimum order requirement.

    Can I modify or cancel my order?

    You can modify or cancel your order within 5 minutes of placing it by messaging us on WhatsApp. Once food preparation has started, modifications may not be possible. We aim to be flexible and will do our best to accommodate your requests whenever feasible.

    Still have questions?

    Our friendly team is here to help! Reach out via WhatsApp and we'll get back to you as soon as possible.
